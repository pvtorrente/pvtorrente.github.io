Who Am I?

My name is Peter Torrente. I graduated from Lafayette College in May 2020 with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Film and Media Studies. For my capstone, I researched how the methods of creating matte paintings have changed, but the way these images are perceived by viewers have not. I have worked on a variety of multimedia projects ranging from working in Rome, Italy at Romeing Magazine creating video content for their social media promoting upcoming articles, to interviewing Grammy Award-winning American musician, Printz Board at MLB Advanced Media, to working on Jaguar's 2020 IPACE commericals.

Filming and video editing is not where my skill set ends. I also have experience using DAWs, such as Pro Tools and Ableton Live, in real studio and live settings. Also, I have helped record and produce live recordings and mixings of concerts with artists, such as George Clanton and Negative Gemini. Lastly, I have taught myself HTML and CSS while working on WJRH.org, my college radio station's website.
